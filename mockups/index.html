💡 Recommendations

For meeting-focused use: Option A (Bottom Flyout) keeps transcription visible without splitting horizontal editor space, and the folder view is always accessible.

For versatility: Option C (Three-Panel with Dock) offers the most flexibility, letting users dock transcription bottom OR right based on preference.

For simplicity: Option B (Floating Widget) maintains a familiar feel but makes transcription less intrusive.

For deep work: Option D (Focus Mode) provides maximum writing focus with contextual panels that appear only when needed.
